Filipino Income Dashboard

This dashboard shows the top and bottom highest earning
region in the Philippines and also displays the socio-political
landscape of the country when it comes to gender using
PowerBI and its data manipulating tool called DAX.

Bicycle Rental Business Dashboard

This dashboard shows the most suitable countries and cities
to establish a bicycle rental Business with taking into account
the temperature, GDP per capita, and population of the country
and city. I used the DAX function to manipulate the data and
used PowerBI as the software to create the dashboard.
